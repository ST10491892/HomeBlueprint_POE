Our Story

Home BluePrint is a modern architectural and design studio base in Johannesburg, South Africa. We are dedicated to creating innovative, sustainable, and practical designs. Since our founding in 2025, we've transformed countless spaces into inspiring environments that balance functionality and beauty.

Our philosophy is simple – design with purpose, precision, and passion. Every project, no matter the size, receives our full dedication and expertise.

Meet the team

Nhlakanipho Ntshangase - Founder & Lead Designer

Founder of Home BluePrint Studio, Nhlakanipho Ntshangase is a qualified architectural designer with a diploma in Architectural Technology from DUT in Durban. His academic foundation in sustainable and modern design principles has shaped his unique approach - merging creativity with technical precision. Over the years, he has worked on a range of residential, commercial, and urban projects, each reflecting his deep commitment to innovation, functionality, and aesthetics. Nhlakanipho's ability to translate ideas into timeless spaces has earned him recognition among clients who value integrity, attention to detail, and personalized design.

Felicia Ntshangase - Accountant

Felicia Ntshangase oversees the finacial operations of Home BluePrint Studio with professionalism and precision, She earned her Bachelor of Commerce in Accounting from the University of Johannesburg, where she specialized in business finance and project budgeting. Felicia manages finacial planning, client invoicing, and project cost control, ensuring every project is executed within the budget and with full transparency. Her expertise supports the studio's long-term growth and helps maintain strong client trust

Lwazi Nyembe - Design Assistant

Lwazi Nyembe serves as the Design Assistant at Home BluePrint Studio, contributing both technical skill and creative insight to the firm's project. He holds a Certificate in Architectural Drafting from Tshwane South TVET College, where he developed strong skills in CAD software, 3D visualization, and building documentation. Lwazi works closely with Nhlakanipho in developing concept plans, refining layouts, and ensuring that every design meets the studios high standards. His reliability, eagerness to learn, and problem-solving mindset make him a valued member of the team.
